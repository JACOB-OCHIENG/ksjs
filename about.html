üëë Principal

Mrs. Grace Akinyi

Principal & Founder

25+ Years Experience
2015 Founded School

Mrs. Akinyi is a retired primary school teacher with over 25 years of experience in education. She holds a Diploma in Primary Education from Kenyatta University and has served in various schools across Homa Bay County before establishing King Solomon Junior.

"Education is the foundation of a better future. At King Solomon Junior, we believe every child deserves quality education regardless of their background."

Key Achievements

  • Established school from 15 to 180+ students
  • Implemented CBC curriculum successfully
  • Built strong community partnerships
