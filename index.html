Our Mission

To provide quality, holistic education that nurtures every child's potential, fostering academic excellence, character development, and community responsibility.

Our Vision

To be the leading primary school in Homa Bay, recognized for academic excellence, character formation, and community impact.

Our Values

  • Respect: For self, others, and the environment
  • Excellence: In academics, character, and service
  • Community: Building strong relationships and partnerships
